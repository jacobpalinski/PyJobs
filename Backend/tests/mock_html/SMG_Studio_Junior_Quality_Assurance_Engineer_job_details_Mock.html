We are seeking a highly motivated Junior Quality Assurance Engineer to join our game development team. As a QA engineer, you will work closely with our development teams to ensure the highest level of quality is achieved for our games.


Responsibilities:


  • Test games and ensure they meet design specifications.
  • Report bugs and issues to the development team.
  • Work with the development team to reproduce and fix issues.
  • Create and maintain test plans and test cases.
  • Collaborate with other departments to ensure that quality standards are met.
  • Participate in the development process to provide feedback on potential issues.


Requirements:


  • Experience with game engines such as Unity
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Ability to understand and pick up new tools easily.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple tasks simultaneously
  • Confidence to communicate ideas, solutions and issues clearly.


Preferred qualifications and experience:


  • Job experience in game development, QA testing or JIRA.
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related experience.
  • Knowledge of programming languages such as C#, php or Python.
  • A passion for and good understanding of board games and their mechanics.
  • Familiarity with test automation tools.
  • Knowledge of Xcode and Android Studio.
  • Understanding of GIT and managing repositories.


We welcome all applicants who are passionate about gaming and software development. If you are a highly motivated individual who thrives in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment, we would love to hear from you.
